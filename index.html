What is RothConv?

RothConv helps professionals and individual investors compare inside‑funded versus outside‑funded conversions, quantify tax‑rate‑spread profit, and fund the embedded Synthetic Roth Contribution when taxes are paid with cash.

  • Bracket‑aware projections with inflation, Social Security, and horizon controls
  • Decomposition of returns into Core vs. Synthetic components
  • Downloadable tables suitable for client reports

Who is it for?

RIAs, planners, and analytically minded investors who want transparent mechanics rather than black‑box heuristics.
